Activity 1 – Beijing Tourist Brochure

Introduction

For this activity, I created a virtual brochure about the most important tourist attractions in Beijing. I used Conditional Type 1 sentences to describe future experiences and recommendations for visitors.

Tourist Attractions

  • Great Wall of China
  • Forbidden City
  • Temple of Heaven
  • Summer Palace
  • Tiananmen Square

Conditional Type 1

  • If you visit the Great Wall, you will enjoy amazing views.
  • If you travel to Beijing, you will discover Chinese culture.
  • If you visit the Forbidden City, you will learn about Chinese history.
  • If you go to the Summer Palace, you will take beautiful photos.
  • If you visit Tiananmen Square, you will see important monuments.
  • If you try Chinese food, you will discover new flavors.
  • If you visit Beijing in spring, you will enjoy pleasant weather.
  • If you explore the Temple of Heaven, you will learn about ancient traditions.
  • If you travel with friends, you will have a wonderful experience.
  • If you visit Beijing, you will create unforgettable memories.
